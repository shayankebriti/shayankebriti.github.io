FractMorph

Deformable registration Transformer Reproducible research

Multi-domain transformer framework for deformable medical image registration (preprint available; journal revision ongoing, Medical Image Analysis, IF 11.8).

Summary

  • Problem: robust multi-modal deformable registration under anatomical variability.
  • Contribution: spatial + fractional Fourier domain transformer with full training and ablation pipeline.
  • Evidence: competitive benchmarks and open-source reproducible implementation.

Perfusion MRI Analysis Toolkit

Clinical software BET segmentation Quantitative imaging

Clinical-facing perfusion MRI pipeline designed for real-world deployment.

Summary

  • Problem: reliable perfusion quantification in heterogeneous tumour cases.
  • Contribution: preprocessing, BET-based segmentation, ROI tools, and quantitative perfusion metrics.
  • Evidence: validated metrics via independent numerical integration and clinical cross-checks.

Availability

Cardiac Mesh Reconstruction & MedMesh

3D anatomy GNN supervision In-silico trials

Structure-aware template-based deformation for automated 3D cardiac mesh reconstruction, supported by MedMesh dataset generation tooling.

Summary

  • Problem: CNN-only feature extraction lacks global anatomical context.
  • Contribution: GNN-based segmentation supervision capturing local and global structure.
  • Evidence: reproducible dataset generation and mesh processing software.
